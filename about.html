OUR HISTORY

The company’s founder Eric Chatelain started ABP Construction after working as an industry-recognized roof consultant on roofing projects for large corporations, the federal government, and municipalities. Eric recognized the need for a higher level of service and professionalism in the roofing industry. As a result, he founded ABP Construction with a dedication to unrivaled workmanship and unparalleled customer service.
